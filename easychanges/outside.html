When outside...

If you are planning to go on a walk, run, etc. always wear a mask. A mask will allow you to take protective precautions and reduce your risk of getting infected.

Stay 6ft

If you are walking with family members outside, stay at least 6ft from others. By keeping this distance you will decrease your likelyhood to transmit the virus to others.

It is important to recognize that social distancing is for necessary activies -- for example the grocery store or excercise. We must engage cautiously in order to protect the workers on the frontlines.

Mental health!

This time is incredibly scary. Be sure to check in with your loved one and talk to professionals if you are going through a rough time. Don't be afraid to reach out for help.
